| SuperAdmin | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Admin | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Developer | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Tester | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Viewer | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
